    How to arrive?

    From Oaxaca to Mexico City

    From Mexico City to Oaxaca

    By car, take the 190 highway towards Mexico city; you will pass one toll and in the second one take the exit to Nochixtlan. You will start driving along the Latinoamerican road; Yahuitlán is approximately 15 minutos far from Nochixtlan on Mexico city direction. It should take around 1hr 15 mins driving from Oaxaca.

    De Ciudad de Mexico hacia Oaxaca

    By car, take the 190 highway towards Oaxaca; you will drive a long straight road called Amozoc through the outskirts of Puebla (do not forget to take pictures of the volcanes, Popocatepetl and Iztazihuatl), once you have passed two tolls take the exit to Oaxaca, carry on through the highway and take the exit on the Nochixtlan toll. The exit will lead you to the Latinoamerican road towards Yahuitlán which is around 15 mins towards Mexico city direction. It should take around 4hr 15 mins driving from Mexico City.

    Ruta Mixteca

    The main attraction in Sto. Domingo Yanhuitlan is its magnificent church and its beautiful ex-convent. There are also a couple of nature activities and bushwalks in Las Conchas and Los Alamos. Yanhuitlan is part of the Ruta Mixteca, so it is a great place to stay one night and start the travel for the rest of the day.

    What to eat?

    Oaxaca is well known for its traditional and delicious food so you cannot miss to try some delicious tlayudas with quesillo or eat one of its nine mole types, amarillo is one of the famous one, you can have an amarillo quesadilla; if you are more adventurous you can try grasshopers with salt and lemon with a mezcal. In the morning you can have water chocolate and a Oaxacan tamal.
